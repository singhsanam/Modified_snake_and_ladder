Game Rules

On each move you will be shown the Shortest Path📈 to reach Destination

Before clicking on "Start Game" You can Customize the positions of Snake and Ladders by dragging them.

No two elements are allowed to have the same starting point be it Snake🐉 or Ladder🪜 to avoid ambiguities

Once your game has started you will no longer have the freedom to reposition the Snakes🐉 and Ladders🪜.

Have Fun👻 by creating loops 🔂 using Snakes🐉 and Ladders🪜 .
